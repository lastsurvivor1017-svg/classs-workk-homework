Who We Are

ComputerOnline.tj is Tajikistan's premier destination for high-quality computers, electronics, and unparalleled technical services. Founded with a vision to bridge the technological gap and empower individuals and businesses, we have grown to become a trusted name in the industry. Our commitment extends beyond just sales; we strive to provide complete solutions and reliable support.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make cutting-edge technology accessible to everyone in Tajikistan. We are dedicated to offering a diverse range of products, from powerful laptops and desktops to essential accessories, all while ensuring exceptional customer service and expert technical assistance. We believe in fostering a technologically advanced community.
